## Getting Started

### What is SyncTracker?

SyncTracker is your comprehensive music sync licensing management system. It helps you:
- Organize your music catalog
- Track sync licensing deals from pitch to payment
- Manage relationships with music supervisors and clients
- Monitor revenue and payments
- Create professional documents (quotes, contracts)
- Never miss a deadline

### First Time Login

1. Open your web browser and go to the SyncTracker URL (provided by your admin)
2. Log in with your credentials
3. You'll land on the **Dashboard** - your command center

## Managing Your Music Catalog

### Adding a New Song

1. Click **"Songs"** in the left menu
2. Click **"+ Add Song"** button
3. Fill in the details:

   **Basic Information**
   - Title (required)
   - Artist (required)
   - Album
   - Genre
   - Mood (e.g., upbeat, emotional, intense)
   - Tempo/BPM

   **Ownership Information** (Important!)
   - Publishing Ownership % (your share of publishing)
   - Master Ownership % (your share of master recording)
   - Composer/Publisher details
   - Artist/Label details

   **Metadata** (helps with pitching)
   - Description
   - Tags (keywords for searching)
   - Lyrics (if applicable)

4. Click **"Save"**

### Editing Song Details

1. Go to **Songs**
2. Find the song (use search if needed)
3. Click on the song title
4. Click **"Edit"**
5. Make your changes
6. Click **"Update"**

### Organizing with Playlists

Create playlists to organize songs by:
- Client/Music Supervisor
- Mood or Genre
- Project Type (TV, Film, Commercial)
- Season (Holiday, Summer, etc.)

## Working with Deals

### The Deal Pipeline

Every sync deal moves through these stages:

1. **New Request** - Initial inquiry received
2. **Pending Approval** - Waiting for internal approval
3. **Quoted** - Quote sent to client
4. **Use Confirmed** - Client confirmed they'll use the song
5. **Being Drafted** - Contract is being prepared
6. **Out for Signature** - Waiting for signed contract
7. **Payment Received** - Payment has been received
8. **Completed** - Deal is finished

### Creating a New Deal

1. Click **"Deals"** in the left menu
2. Click **"+ Add Deal"**
3. Fill in the project information:

   **Project Details**
   - Project Name (e.g., "Nike Summer Campaign")
   - Episode Number (if applicable)
   - Project Type (TV, Film, Commercial, etc.)
   - Project Description

   **Song & Contact**
   - Select the song from your catalog
   - Select the contact (music supervisor/client)

   **Usage Details**
   - Usage (background, featured, opening credits, etc.)
   - Media (TV, Film, Streaming, etc.)
   - Territory (Worldwide, North America, etc.)
   - Term (Perpetual, 5 years, etc.)
   - Exclusivity (Yes/No)

   **Financial**
   - Deal Value (total fee)
   - Our Fee (your portion after splits)
   - Ballpark range (for tracking)

4. Click **"Create Deal"**

### Updating Deal Status

1. Go to **Deals**
2. Click on the deal
3. Use the **"Update Status"** dropdown
4. Select the new status
5. The date will be automatically recorded

### Adding Notes to a Deal

- Click on any deal
- Scroll to **"Notes"** section
- Add important information like:
  - Follow-up reminders
  - Client feedback
  - Special requests
  - Internal notes

## Pitching Songs

### Creating a Pitch

1. Navigate to a **Deal**
2. Click **"Create Pitch"**
3. Add:
   - Select songs to pitch
   - Submission date (automatically set)
   - Notes (why this song fits)

### Following Up on Pitches

1. Go to **"Pitches"** in the menu
2. View all pitches and their status:
   - **Pending**: Waiting for response
   - **Responded**: Client has responded
   - **No Response**: Need to follow up

3. Set follow-up dates to stay organized

### Bulk Pitching

For pitching multiple songs to one project:

1. Create a **Playlist** with candidate songs
2. Go to **"Bulk Pitches"**
3. Select your playlist
4. Select the contact
5. Choose an email template
6. Send

## Tracking Revenue

### Deal Financial Details

For each deal, track:
- **Full Song Value**: Total sync fee for the song
- **Our Fee**: Your share after splits
- **Full Recording Fee**: Total for master recording
- **Our Recording Fee**: Your share of recording

### Payment Tracking

1. When a deal reaches "Payment Received":
   - Enter the **Payment Date**
   - Verify the **Amount Received**
   - Add any **Payment Notes**

### Income Dashboard

Go to **"Income"** to see:
- Monthly revenue breakdown
- Revenue by project type
- Revenue by song
- Payment status (received vs pending)
- Year-to-date totals

### Generating Reports

1. Click **"Reports"** in the menu
2. Choose date range
3. Export as:
   - PDF
   - Excel
   - CSV

## Common Workflows

### Workflow 1: New Sync Opportunity

1. Receive inquiry from music supervisor
2. **Add Contact** (if new)
3. **Create Deal** with project details
4. **Select Song(s)** from catalog
5. **Create Pitch** record
6. **Send Quote** via email
7. **Update Status** as deal progresses
8. **Add to Calendar**: Air date, payment due date
9. **Track Payment** when received
10. **Mark as Completed**

### Workflow 2: Building a Pitch Playlist

1. Receive brief from client
2. **Create Playlist** for the project
3. **Search Catalog** by genre/mood/tempo
4. **Add Candidate Songs** to playlist
5. **Review** and refine selection
6. **Create Bulk Pitch** from playlist
7. **Send** to client
8. **Track Responses**

### Workflow 3: Monthly Review

1. Go to **"Reports"**
2. Generate **Revenue Report** for month
3. Review **Deal Pipeline**
4. Check **Payment Status**
5. Update **Pending Deals**
6. Plan **Follow-ups** for next month
7. **Archive** completed deals

## Glossary

**Deal**: A sync licensing opportunity or agreement
**Pitch**: Submitting a song for consideration
**Master**: The recorded version of a song
**Publishing**: The composition/songwriting rights
**Sync Fee**: One-time payment for using a song
**Territory**: Geographic area where song can be used
**Term**: Duration of the license
**Ballpark**: Estimated deal value range
**Use Type**: How the song is used (background, featured, etc.)
